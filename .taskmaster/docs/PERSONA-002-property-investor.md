## Goals & Motivations

### Primary Goals

1. **Portfolio-Wide Visibility**
   - See all projects at a glance with status and timeline
   - Quick context switch without losing progress
   - Compare investment vs. personal project priorities

2. **Context Preservation Across Projects**
   - Never re-explain property details to AI
   - Pick up exactly where left off, even weeks later
   - Consistent quality outputs without manual context management

3. **ROI-Optimized Design Decisions**
   - Know which upgrades yield highest returns
   - Compare renovation cost vs. ARV (After Repair Value)
   - Market-specific design trends for target buyer/renter

4. **Delegate with Confidence**
   - Generate professional materials for contractors
   - Clear specifications to prevent scope creep
   - Visual standards that teams can execute

5. **Speed of Execution**
   - Minutes, not hours, to visualize options
   - Quick iteration on design alternatives
   - Instant estimates for budget planning

### Secondary Goals

- Impress potential buyers/renters with quality staging
- Document property conditions for insurance/legal
- Build a library of successful design templates
- Scale the business without proportional time increase

### Specific Frustrations

1. **"Every time I open ChatGPT for a new project, I start from zero"**
   - AI doesn't remember property details
   - Manual copy-paste of context
   - Inconsistent prompt quality

2. **"I have 50 tabs open with inspiration for different properties"**
   - No unified place for multi-property design research
   - Can't associate inspiration with specific rooms/properties

3. **"My contractor texts me photos and asks 'is this right?' and I can't remember what I asked for"**
   - Design decisions scattered across messages
   - No single source of truth

4. **"I know what $30K buys in Austin but I have no idea for my Denver property"**
   - Market-specific pricing opacity
   - Generic estimates don't account for location

5. **"I spend more time explaining what I want than actually deciding"**
   - Repetitive communication overhead
   - Wish AI could just "know" the project
