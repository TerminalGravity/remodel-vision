## Goals & Motivations

### Primary Goals

1. **Visualize Before Committing**
   - See realistic renderings of design ideas before purchasing materials
   - Avoid "buyer's remorse" on major decisions (cabinets, countertops, flooring)
   - Compare multiple options side-by-side

2. **Stay Within Budget**
   - Track costs as design evolves
   - Get alerts when choices exceed budget
   - Find alternatives that achieve similar looks for less

3. **Make Confident Decisions**
   - Understand what will work in their specific space
   - Validate that color/material combinations look good together
   - Confirm measurements before ordering

4. **Plan the Project Realistically**
   - Understand the sequence of work
   - Know what can be DIY vs. needs a pro
   - Create a timeline that fits their life

### Secondary Goals

- Impress spouse/partner with professional-looking designs
- Learn about materials and trends
- Avoid the stress of dealing with contractors for small decisions
- Have a clear plan to show contractors for quotes

### Specific Frustrations

1. **"I found the perfect cabinet on Instagram but I don't know if it fits my space"**
   - Inspiration disconnected from their actual room dimensions

2. **"Every design app wants me to start from scratch"**
   - Doesn't want to draw floor plans manually
   - Wants to upload their home and start designing

3. **"I spent 3 hours choosing a backsplash and still don't know if it'll look good"**
   - Can't see materials in context of their actual kitchen

4. **"My contractor asked what I want and I couldn't explain it"**
   - No professional renderings to share
   - Ends up showing random Pinterest photos

5. **"I ordered the wrong size island"**
   - Measurements were off
   - Didn't account for door swings, walkways

## Feature Prioritization for DIYers

### Must Have (P0)

| Feature | Why Critical |
|---------|--------------|
| Photo-to-3D conversion | Entry point, proves value immediately |
| Style-matched inspiration | Replaces Pinterest with personalized results |
| Real-time material preview | Core visualization need |
| Budget tracking | Prevents overspending |
| Before/After comparison | Validates design decisions |

### Should Have (P1)

| Feature | Why Important |
|---------|---------------|
| Product linking (SKUs) | Bridges design to purchasing |
| Measurement validation | Prevents ordering errors |
| Contractor export | Enables professional communication |
| Save/version designs | Tracks decision evolution |

### Nice to Have (P2)

| Feature | Why Desirable |
|---------|---------------|
| AR preview | See changes in real space |
| Community gallery | Inspiration from similar homes |
| Contractor marketplace | End-to-end solution |
| Timeline planner | Project management |

## Appendix: Interview Questions for Validation

1. Walk me through your last home improvement project. What was hardest?
2. How do you currently find inspiration for your home?
3. Have you ever regretted a design decision? What happened?
4. How do you share design ideas with your partner or contractor?
5. What would make you pay for a design tool?
6. How important is seeing realistic renderings vs. "good enough"?
7. Would you trust AI-generated design suggestions?
